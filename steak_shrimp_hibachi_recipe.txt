Steak & Shrimp Hibachi

Source: @creatorhandle

Servings: 2
Serving Size: 1 plate

## Ingredients
- Steak [meat]
- Shrimp [seafood]
- Thai Chili Pepper Jack cheese [dairy]
- Homemade Teriyaki sauce [condiments]
- Spicy Yum Yum sauce [condiments]

## Instructions
1. Prepare the Thai Chili Pepper Jack Wontons.
2. Prep the steak and shrimp for hibachi grilling.
3. Add homemade sauces including Spicy Yum Yum sauce along with the teriyaki.

## Tips
- Enjoy with Smoked Sage Paloma in a highball glass.
