Drunken Sailor

Source: @nerdytavern

Servings: 1

## Ingredients
- 2 oz rum [bar]
- 1 oz lime juice [bar]
- 1 oz simple syrup [bar]
- garnish: lime wheel [produce]

## Instructions
1. In a shaker, combine the rum, lime juice, and simple syrup with ice. 
2. Shake well and strain into a glass filled with ice.
3. Garnish with a lime wheel.

## Tips
- Serve in a rocks glass or any suitable cocktail glass.
- For extra flavor, consider adding a dash of bitters.
