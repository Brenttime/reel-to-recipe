Drunken Sailor

Source: @nerdytavern

Servings: 1

## Macros
Calories: N/A | Protein: N/A | Carbs: N/A | Fat: N/A

## Ingredients
- 1 part rum [bar]
- 1 part lime juice [bar]
- 1 part soda water [beverages]
- Dashes of bitters [spices]  

## Instructions
1. Combine rum, lime juice, and bitters in a shaker filled with ice.
2. Shake well until chilled and strain into a glass with ice.
3. Top with soda water and garnish with a lime wheel.

## Tips
- Serve in a highball glass.
- For added sweetness, you can include a dash of simple syrup.
